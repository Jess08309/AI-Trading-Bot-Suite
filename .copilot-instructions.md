### 📊 Trading Logic Flow
1. Fetch prices for all 10 pairs every 180 seconds
2. Calculate buy score: ML + RL + Meta + sentiment + volatility + early bonus
3. Buy if score >= 3/15
4. Monitor with trailing stop (0.8% from max)
5. Sell triggers: profit target, stop loss, trailing stop, ML signal, RL signal
6. Update RL Q-table and Meta Learner on trade close
7. Auto-save AI models every 5 trades
